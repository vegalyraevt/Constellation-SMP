Server Events and Future Plans

Pictured to the left is the Evolutionary stone hunt event plaza from the day one event. We plan to have a wide variety of solo and team events for the server! You can expect a event each holiday along with other events in between.

There will also be montly tournaments for the server. These tournaments will decide on the server rankings for the elleite four and the champion. The tournaments will be a double elimination bracket. The winner of the tournament will be the champion and will have to defend their title in the next tournament. The remaining pLayers in the top five will be the elite four.
